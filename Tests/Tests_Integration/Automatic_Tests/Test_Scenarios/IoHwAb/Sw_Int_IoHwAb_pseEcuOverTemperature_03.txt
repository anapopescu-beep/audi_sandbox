If the {ECU Temperature} is greater or equal to {NVP_s16TemperatureSensorHighThrs} then the {Autotest Result} must be NOK. If the {ECU Temperature} is greater or equal to {NVP_s16TemperatureSensorHighThrs} then the {Autotest Result} must be NOK.
ARCH_SW_pclEcuOverTemperature: This is the autotest to check over temperature of MCU.
